# Day 98 - Space Race Data Analysis

## Overview
Comprehensive analysis of synthetic space mission data from 1957-2020 covering 12 countries and 9 mission types. Visualizations include time series, choropleth maps, sunburst charts, and cost analysis.

## Key Concepts
- Multi-faceted data storytelling with synthetic data
- px.choropleth() for geographic distribution
- px.sunburst() for hierarchical mission type breakdown
- Time series with fill_between and rolling windows
- Cost analysis across countries and decades

## Reflection
The space race data tells a clear story: US and USSR dominate early, China enters later, and mission success rates climb steadily from ~80% in the 1960s to ~95% in the 2010s. The sunburst chart revealed that "Satellite" missions account for 30% of all launches — the unsung workhorse of space exploration.
